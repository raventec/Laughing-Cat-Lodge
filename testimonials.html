Guy Marchi, owner and proprietor of the Laughing Cat Lodge, has provided excellent care for my cats on numerous occasions. My cats were picked up and dropped off conveniently by Guy himself. Most importantly, I never once felt concerned or worried while I was away on business or vacation as I whole heartedly could put my trust into the caring hands of Guy's expertise with my cats at the Laughing Cat Lodge, formerly the Spoiled Cat Rainforest Retreat.

I would personally recommend Guy's service at the Laughing Cat Lodge to any discerning cat owner.

Thanks Guy for your past service and all the times you will continue to care for my "babies" in the future!
