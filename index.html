Welcome

The Laughing Cat Lodge welcomes your cat into our home. Your cat will start in a suite in the cat room. Once comfortable, usually within the hour, he can roam the cat room which includes perches, toys, bird and bug videos and tunnels. Once comfortable there he will be free to roam in the safety and comfort of our house. We only take a couple of cats at a time as we love their company and hopefully they will love ours. My name is Guy Marchi. My nickname is "Cat Man Do". I was the founder/owner of a larger facility that was sold to my good friends Jill and Barry.
